#+title: Visualizing your effort over time using org mode


One thing that makes video games addictive is how immediately you can see feedback. Life seems to be typically be about lots of small improvements accrued over a long period of time... and then occasionally all of those things coming together and you feeling like "I did it!".

What if you could get some positive feedback on those small wins and see your improvements more immediately?

One way to do this is using [[file:20210323175953-orgstat.org][orgstat]], a [[file:20210323175936-haskell.org][Haskell]] based statistics visualizer for org-mode
